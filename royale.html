Royale


Food. Games. Pyjamas. Royale. Annddddd we’re back !! CTA is kicking this year off with ROYALE, a slumber party themed evening! Take a break from your stressful studies and kick it with us for a casual night of giant lifesize board games, arts and crafts, casual lounging and FREE FOOD!

Come down to enjoy the laid back sleepy vibes with all your chillest of friends - we’ve jam packed the night with game shows, board games of all shapes and sizes, snacks, movies, photo booths, raffles and much, much more. Bring your bathrobes, onesies, slippers; anything you feel comfortable in. We’ll make sure you feel relaxed and have a good time !!
